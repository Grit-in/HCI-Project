Terry Bogard

Terry Bogard is a versatile character with excellent neutral game and strong pressure options. His Power Wave and Burn Knuckle provide solid zoning tools.

Terry Guide PDF Frame Data Sheet

Character Overview

Strengths

  • Excellent neutral game
  • Strong pressure options
  • Good zoning tools
  • Solid anti-air options

Weaknesses

  1. Limited defensive options
  2. Vulnerable to cross-ups
  3. Requires meter for optimal damage
  4. Can be predictable
